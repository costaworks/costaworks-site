What's not included

Third-party costs — materials, prototyping, software licenses, vendor fees — are passed through at cost. I'll flag these before they happen. No surprises on the invoice.

Contract rates

For longer engagements or retainer arrangements, custom rates are available. If hourly doesn't fit what you need, let's talk.

Hours and flexibility

The weekly ranges are a guide to the working relationship, not a rigid billing threshold. I bill for actual work done, not arbitrary minimums.
